    Cactus Hunt

    by Peter Mathijssen
    published on February 10, 2019

    There are three sizes of cactuses. Depending on which one you click you get 1, 2 or 3 points. Pressing reset restarts the count at zero.

    The phone screams when you touch a cactus. Phones can’t deliver an electric shock yet, else that would have been an option. ;-)

    You are allowed to use the sounds and images.

    All images are from www.pixabay.com and are in the public domain.

    Sounds are from https://freesound.org/people/jorickhoofd/sounds/180345/ with attribution https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/3.0/
